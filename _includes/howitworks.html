How it Works

TYP is an online digital learning portfolio which allows students to communicate, evidence and reflect on their learning journey.

Using TYP students can:

  1. record, plan and reflect on their learning
  2. organise their digital learning files
  3. showcase their digital learning outcomes with their subject teacher.
  4. receive messages from their teacher

To get started, a teacher simply signs up and invites their students to join their school using a School Code which displayed on log-in.
