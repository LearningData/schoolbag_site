Benefits

Personal Dashboard

From their personal dashboard teachers and students can view their timetable and homework activity. The dashboard gives an overview of what needs to be completed and any other important information communicated through the events planner or noticeboard.

Record Homework

Both students and teachers can assign or record homework tasks. Teachers can easily attach a resource they wish students to use to complete their work. Schoolbag tracks the progress and lets the teacher know when work is ready for review.

Review Homework

Options available to review work either directly in Schoolbag or Microsoft OneNote with just a simple click. Schoolbag stores all completed and reviewed work should users wish to access this at a later date.
