Benefits

Framework for self-management

The portfolio provides a framework for self-management in the self-directed TY learning environment.

Comprehensive record

It supports all aspects of the TY curriculum and provides a comprehensive record of the personal efforts and achievements of each student.

Portfolio work

Students can share selected collections of work from different subjects with their subject teachers.

Mobile Responsive

Students can update their portfolio on a phone, tablet or desktop device.

Intuitive

The intuitive and user friendly interface means that teachers and students adapt to the system quickly.

Permanently Accessible

As the application is cloud based, files are permanently available once students have an internet connection.
